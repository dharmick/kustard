ABOUT

Holdingwilley is a cricket website named after brian Johnston's famous quote during an England vs West Indies Test Match at The Oval in 1976 which said "The Bowler's Holding , the Batsman's Willey". It offers a special experience of enjoying live shows with unique game analysis tools like COW , Form Factor, Dominance, etc., Tongue-in-cheek cartoons, some wacky tweets & passionate articles from writters across the world.
